Power Cut Map

The map below shows all power cut incidents that we know about across our region, along with a breakdown summary by county and region as of the time shown below. Click on the County Filter to view specific information for your county. The green markers on the map represent a power cut incident that we’re aware of in that area. For more details about the incident, click on the green marker.

Enter your postcode to search for power cuts in your area.

We serve customers across the Midlands, South West England and South Wales. To check who distributes the electricity where you are, go to our ‘Find your distributor’ page and enter your postcode.

We have also identified customers affected by power cuts who are registered on our Priority Services Register. We will proactive call all Priority Services customers affected by a power cut as soon as possible and help them where necessary. For more details, visit our Priority Services pages.
